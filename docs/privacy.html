BalauDeck — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026

BalauDeck is an SSH, SFTP, SSH-tunnel and MySQL/MariaDB client. It is a tool you point at your own servers. This policy explains what it does with your data.

Data we collect

None. BalauDeck has no backend and no account. We do not collect, transmit, store, sell, or share any personal data. There is no analytics, tracking, advertising, or telemetry.

Data stored on your device

  • Connection profiles (host, port, user, folders, saved queries) are stored locally in the app's private storage.
  • Secrets (passwords, private keys, passphrases) are stored in your device's secure keychain (Apple Keychain on iOS/macOS; the equivalent secure store on other platforms).
  • Biometrics (Face ID / Touch ID) are used only locally, by the operating system, to unlock the app. BalauDeck never sees your biometric data.

Network connections

BalauDeck connects only to the servers you configure (SSH/SFTP hosts, database servers, tunnel endpoints). That traffic goes directly from your device to your servers — never through us. We operate no servers that receive your data.

Backups you create

The optional "Sync connections" feature produces an encrypted backup file (AES-256-GCM, protected by a passphrase you choose). You control that file entirely — it is shared only where and when you choose to move it.

Children

BalauDeck is a developer/administration tool and is not directed at children.
